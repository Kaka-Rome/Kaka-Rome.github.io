About

Hello! I'm Kaka, and I'm from West Java, Indonesia. I got my start as a Designer by creating vector art. Since 2020 I started it all. I began designing vector for wall décor, design for t-shirts, stickers, printed on wood and other types of printing needs. I am open to all kinds of vector work, like Animals vector, Persons vector, Vehicles vector, etc. I would love to work with a brand that shares these values. I am also open to long-term partnership! If you like my work or if you have any question, please feel free to reach out by filling in the contact form!
